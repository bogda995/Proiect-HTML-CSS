artist statement

During my Bachelor's degree, I embarked on a journey of exploration, immersing myself in a myriad of artistic techniques. As a Visual Artist, my primary goal is to unearth the most suitable mediums for expressing my innermost thoughts and emotions, while simultaneously showcasing the rich tapestry of possibilities within the artistic realm.

My creative process begins with a keen eye for subjects that resonate with me, meticulously documenting their essence before translating them into the visual language.

While I hold a deep appreciation for traditional techniques, my artistic evolution thrives on the fusion of diverse modes of expression to achieve the desired outcome. What truly drives me in each project is the ability to seamlessly integrate these various approaches, ensuring that every viewer experiences a profound connection with my art.
